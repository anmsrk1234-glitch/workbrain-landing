FAQ

Questions answered

Especially the one you're already thinking about.

Why not just use Notion AI connectors?
+
Notion AI connectors only work if you already live in Notion — and even then, they search one tool at a time. WorkBrain is tool-agnostic and searches semantically across all your sources simultaneously. A decision buried in a Slack thread, a spec in Notion, and a blocker in Linear all surface in a single synthesized answer. Plus, you don't have to migrate anything.
Is my data stored on your servers?
+
WorkBrain stores only encrypted semantic embeddings — never the raw content of your documents or messages. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. You can revoke access at any time and your indexes are permanently deleted within 24 hours.
How is this different from ChatGPT with plugins?
+
ChatGPT with plugins requires you to know which plugin to use, trigger it manually, and it still doesn't combine context across sources. WorkBrain is always-on, indexes everything proactively, and synthesizes answers from across all your tools without you having to think about it.
Will it work for teams or just individuals?
+
We're launching for solo founders, PMs, and individual developers first. Team workspaces with shared memory and access controls are on the roadmap for Q3 2026.
